Parks Poster Map Style

Eli Ganong

The Mapbox style that I have created is called Golden Gate Parks and is inspired by Michael Schwab's graphic designs.

I drew inspiration from this set of posters designed for the various parks in the San Francisco Bay area.

In the map, colors for roads, terrain, and water are taken from the posters. The font used in the labels comes from national parks.

The map reveals more details and stylization as you zoom in.
