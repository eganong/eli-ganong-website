Central American Climate Refugees

This is the culmination of an in depth research project looking at the so called "climate refugee's who have had to flee countries in Central America due to the effects of climate change. This map and its accompanying figures tell the story of failed harvests, drought, migration, and environmental change. Take a look at the full graphic to fully grasp the sitation.
